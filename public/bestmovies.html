The Best Movies according to Chris

My top 3 movies of all time.

The Empire Strikes Back

This is the best in the Star Wars series. The down ending seems to have only the faintest glimmer of hope for the Rebel Alliance.

The Shawshank Redemption

A story of redemption, but it makes you wonder: "Did Andy DuFrain got away with murder?"

Shoplifters

A film about chosen family, but with a twist.
